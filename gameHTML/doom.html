About this game

DOOM: The Dark Ages thrusts players into a brutal, medieval-inspired world where science fiction meets dark fantasy. Set in a time before the events of DOOM (2016), this origin story follows the rise of the Slayer — humanity’s ultimate weapon against the forces of Hell.


The world is on the brink of annihilation as demonic legions pour into reality, corrupting kingdoms and reducing civilizations to ashes. In the midst of this chaos, a lone warrior emerges from blood-soaked battlefields, clad in ancient armor and wielding devastating weapons fueled by both flesh and steel. His mission is simple: total destruction of the infernal horde.


Players will fight through nightmarish castles, ruined fortresses, and hellish landscapes, using brutal melee weapons, dark-age firearms, and futuristic relics. With relentless combat, massive boss battles, and a heavy-metal aesthetic, DOOM: The Dark Ages delivers a savage blend of myth and mayhem — telling the legend of the Slayer before he became a god of vengeance.
