About this game

Set in a zombie apocalypse, Left 4 Dead 2 (L4D2) is the highly anticipated sequel to Left 4 Dead, the award-winning co-op game of 2008.


This first-person horror shooter takes you and your teammates through the cities, graveyards, and swamps of the Deep South, from Savannah to New Orleans, across five expansive campaigns.


You play as one of four new survivors, armed to the teeth with a wide array of classic and improved weapons. In addition to guns, you can now unleash your fury on the sick with a variety of brutal melee weapons, from chainsaws to axes, and even the deadly frying pan. You'll test these weapons against (or play as in Versus mode) three new, terrifying and terrifying special sick. You'll also encounter five unusual sick, including the terrifying Mudmen. Taking L4D’s intense, action-packed gameplay to the next level is AI Director 2.0. The improved Director can now change the weather you encounter and the routes you take, in addition to adjusting the number of enemies, effects, and sounds to suit your abilities.
