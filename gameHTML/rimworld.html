About this game

RimWorld builds dynamic stories through an AI storyteller that throws unexpected events like pirates, storms, and strangers. You can choose different storytellers—some chaotic, some calm—to shape your experience.


Colonists are diverse survivors like nobles, farmers, or assassins, each with strengths and limitations. They form and break relationships—falling in love, arguing, or cheating—based on personal traits and daily life.


The game generates a whole planet, from frozen tundras to deadly jungles, each with unique survival challenges. You can form caravans, trade, rescue allies, relocate your base, or raid enemy camps.


Emotions, injuries, and body parts are deeply simulated—every limb and organ matters. Colonists can get prosthetics, organs transplants, or even cybernetic upgrades to turn weakness into strength.
